## About
Applied Research Scientist and Engineer specializing in data science and probabilistic modeling, ML, and AI for sensor-driven systems across automotive, aerospace, and energy domains. Expertise in time-series, anomaly detection, system dynamics and state estimation, diagnostics, and forecasting for uncertainty-aware decision making. Experience across the technology maturity spectrum, from low-TRL research to cloud-deployed models. Hands-on technical lead and IC in research labs, large enterprises, and early-stage startups. Core contributor to open-source research software, PI of funded research proposals at NASA, and recipient of best paper awards.
